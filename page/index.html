Doconomy is a Swedish impact-tech startup that aims to provide both consumers and brands with digital tools to help understand and reduce the climate impact of consumption.

Re-Store is a digital platform that connects carbon-conscious consumers and brands. It aims to level the playing field for climate action through radical symbiotic transparency by requesting that consumers and brands alike disclose their carbon footprint, and as such empower one another to take control of their impact.

When using Re-Store as a user, you start by assessing your individual lifestyle by calculating your climate footprint. Afterward you will receive personalised recommendations on how to cut your emissions and gets presented with products and services within energy, transportation, food and shopping that can help.

The tool is an entirely new kind of e-commerce platform, which inverts the methods used to accelerate consumption to instead ensure more informed and climate-conscious purchase decisions.
