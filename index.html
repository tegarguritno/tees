About
Tegar Guritno

You can call me T. I am a designer based in Indonesia. I got acquainted with design in 2017, and since then, I fell in love with her, then worked for several local companies and started working freelance full time.

I intensely focus on beauty, simplicity, and usability in my designs. I believe that good design not only looks beautiful but also works beautifully. I approach that goal by making sure to listen well and do research, both quantitatively and qualitatively.

Outside of work, I like reading, playing chess, learning new design disciplines, watching movies, trekking, cooking, trying new things, and many other things that just don't feel like enough for me to write here, and well I don't think it's too important either.

Above all, I strive to create a positive environment for collaboration with team members and other stakeholders in developing user-centered products. I hope you're not afraid to contact me. I'm not a serial killer. I'm more like Napoleon Dynamite mixed with McLovin.

Project prices vary depending on the size of the project and the services you choose. I have a variety of services in different price ranges to fit your budget. Project lead times are highly dependent on the size of the project, the services chosen, and how quickly feedback is given, but most of the work, in this case, research, design, and app development, will be completed and online within 2 to 4 weeks.
